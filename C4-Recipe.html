Delicious pancakes

    Ingredients

  • Flour (usually all-purpose)
  • Milk (or buttermilk for fluffier pancakes)
  • Eggs
  • Sugar (optional, for sweetness)
  • Baking powder (helps pancakes rise)
  • Butter or oil (melted, for richness)
  • Vanilla extract (optional, for flavor)

    Instructions

  1. Put a nonstick or cast-iron pan on medium heat (about 2–3 minutes). You want it hot but not smoking.
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, sugar, salt until combined.
  3. In another bowl/jug, whisk milk, egg, melted butter, vanilla.
  4. Combine (don’t overmix!) Pour wet into dry. Whisk just until you no longer see dry flour—small lumps are good.
  5. Rest the batter (optional but great) Let it sit 5 minutes to hydrate and get extra fluffy.
  6. Grease & test the pan Lightly butter/oil the pan. Flick in a drop of batter; if it sizzles gently, you’re ready.
  7. Cook the first side Scoop ¼ cup batter per pancake. Cook 2–3 minutes until bubbles form and edges look set.
  8. Flip Turn once and cook 1–2 minutes more until golden and cooked through.
  9. Repeat & keep warm Adjust heat as needed (too dark = lower heat). Keep finished pancakes on a low oven (90–100°C) if making many.
  10. Serve Top with butter, syrup, fruit, yogurt—your call.

Enjoy your homemade pancakes!
